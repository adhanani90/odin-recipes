French Toast

Description

French toast is a dish made after frying bread soaked in a milk and egg mixture.

It is not a French dish and might have its roots as old as the Roman Empire.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • Â¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the milk, eggs, vanilla, sugar, salt and cinnamon
  2. In a flat frying pan, add some butter
  3. Soak a bread in the mixture and place it on the pan with th ebutter
  4. Turn it on both sides and make sure you don't burn it :)
